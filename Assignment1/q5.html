Title: The Silent Patient

Author: Alex Michael

In the captivating novel, The Silent Patient,the story unfolds through the eyes of a haunted psychotherapist. 
He becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind a mysterious  woman who has stopped speaking after committing a shocking crime. 
As the plot thickens, readers are drawn into a twisted narrative filled with unexpected revelations. 
This gripping tale explores themes of trauma, betrayal, and the complexities of the human mind.
